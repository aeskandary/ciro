Clinical Research Support

CIRO is able to help you with every step of the clinical trial process.

Support Includes & Is Not Limited to:

  • Protocol development
  • EDC and Case Report Form generation
  • Institutional Review Board submission & approval
  • Creation of regulatory binder
  • Site selection
  • Site training
  • On-site or remote monitoring services
  • Data tabulation and clinical study report

CIRO has an established relationship with the New Bolton Center to conduct pre-clinical experiments, including large mammal models.

  • Chronic Disease Models/Microbiome
  • Spine & Longbone
  • Meniscus/Joint Resurfacing
  • Host-Microbial Interactions/Biofilm
  • Acute/Chronic Studies
  • Imaging
